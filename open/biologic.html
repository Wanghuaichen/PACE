BioLogic is Cicada's inductive logic programming (ILP) system for bioinformatics. It collects data from arbitrary public and private bioinformatics sites, maintains a database of examples and background knowledge and runs induction algorithms to generate new knowledge. It presents induced rules as annotated natural language phrases with links to confidence calculations and original sources.
